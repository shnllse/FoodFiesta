Customer Reviews

Mikha Lim

FoodFiesta is a gem of a find in Cabuyao. As a Filipino cuisine enthusiast, I was impressed by the authenticity and quality of the dishes. The Chicken Adobo was cooked to perfection, with just the right balance of soy sauce and vinegar. The ambiance was cozy and inviting, and the staff were friendly and attentive. I highly recommend trying the Lechon Belly—it was crispy and flavorful, just like how my Lola used to make it. FoodFiesta truly offers a taste of home away from home.

mary loi yves

FoodFiesta exceeded my expectations in every way. The Beef Sinigang was absolutely delicious, with tender pork and tangy tamarind broth that warmed me up on a chilly evening. The portion sizes were generous, and the prices were reasonable. I also appreciated the vegetarian options available, such as the Vegetable Kare-Kare, which was flavorful and satisfying. The staff were knowledgeable about the menu and provided excellent recommendations. I'll definitely be returning to FoodFiesta for another culinary adventure.

gwyneth apuli

FoodFiesta is hands down the best Filipino restaurant in town. From the moment I stepped in, I felt like I was transported back to the Philippines. The ambiance was lively and vibrant, and the aroma of foods and spices was mouthwatering. I couldn't resist ordering the Bistek, and it did not disappoint—the meat was tender, and the sauce was rich and flavorful. The Halo-Halo dessert was the perfect way to end the meal, with its refreshing combination of sweet beans, coconut, and ube ice cream. FoodFiesta has definitely earned a permanent spot on my list of favorite restaurants.
